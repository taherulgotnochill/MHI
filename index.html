Who We Are

About MHI

At MHI — the Mental Health Initiative — we believe every young person deserves to feel heard, supported, and understood. We work with youth and young adults to break down the stigma surrounding mental health, providing real resources and support while equipping communities with the education and tools to recognise the signs and start the conversation.

Whether someone is silently struggling or simply looking for guidance, we're here — because mental health matters, and so do you.

I started MHI because I watched people around me struggle without anyone in their corner, and I figured someone had to do something about it. That someone ended up being me. A Mission Born
from Real Life

MHI didn't start in a boardroom. It started with someone who saw the gap, felt the weight of it, and decided to build the bridge they wished had existed.

Arshia brings authenticity, lived experience, and an unshakeable commitment to making mental health support something every young person can reach for — without shame, without barriers.
